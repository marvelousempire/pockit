**Pocket** — Your Back Pocket AI Secretary

Pocket is a personal truth engine that quietly builds an extremely accurate understanding of who you really are.

Instead of asking you to tell it what you want, Pocket continuously collects real data about your behavior, priorities, mood, and daily life through micro-questions, voice conversations, and intuitive inputs. Over time, it builds a deeply accurate personal AI agent that actually knows you — not who you *say* you are, but who you actually are.

### Core Features

- **0.0 to 1.0 Rating System**: All ratings use a precise decimal scale where 1.0 means “Unwavering Yes”
- **Color Wheel Mood Input**: A large, beautiful color spectrum for instant emotional check-ins
- **Micro-Question Engine**: Smart, frequent, short questions about your recent activity (last 30 minutes, last 2 hours, etc.)
- **Voice Journal Agent**: Proactively starts natural voice conversations with you throughout the day, records, transcribes, and turns your words into structured data
- **Consistency Engine**: Quietly validates your answers over time to detect patterns and inconsistencies
- **Long-term Memory Bank**: Stores everything you say and do as structured memory to power your personal AI
- **Personal Data Absorption Agent**: The core AI whose only job is to deeply understand you by constantly collecting truthful data

The ultimate goal of Pocket is to become your personal Back Pocket AI Secretary — an agent that knows you better than most people in your life because it was built from your real behavior and honest daily input.
